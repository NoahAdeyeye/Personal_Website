Writing / Art

My Passion for Writing and Art

I’ve always had a passion for writing and art, nurtured by my love for acting since I was young. I’ve played lead roles in several musicals and plays, embodying characters like Pinocchio and Scar, which ignited my enthusiasm for being on stage. This experience has inspired me to express my creativity through writing and visual arts.

I was fortunate to speak at my high school graduation, where I performed an original poem titled "Victory," highlighting the theme of perseverance during tough times. This moment allowed me to share my voice and passion with others.

In addition to writing, I love digital art, which has fueled my interest in creating animations and YouTube videos in my spare time. Combining these interests allows me to explore storytelling in diverse and exciting ways.
