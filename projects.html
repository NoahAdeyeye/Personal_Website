Empowering Students Through Technology

Tree Technologies is an innovative business I co-founded to connect students with campus information, resources, and tools. Our flagship product, Rooted, is a mobile app currently available for Howard students.

After winning the Howard University Empower pitch competition, we launched Rooted to provide students with easy access to calendars, clubs, student businesses, and sports.
