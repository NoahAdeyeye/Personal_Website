The Journey Begins

I've always been fascinated by technology. From dismantling electronics as a child to creating digital art and animations, my curiosity has driven me to explore multiple disciplines.

At Howard University, I've found the perfect environment to merge my passions for engineering, mathematics, and science. My research experiences at Howard, Caltech, Stanford and NASA JPL have further fueled my ambition to contribute to cutting-edge technologies in integrated photonics.

Beyond academics, I love going to the gym, and reading (especially comics). These outlets provide balance to my technical pursuits and often inspire innovative approaches to my problems.
